# Research Radar

Purpose:

Understand the evolution and future direction of a research field.

Focus on field-level trends rather than individual papers.

# Scope

Responsible for:

- trend analysis
- technology evolution
- landscape mapping

Not responsible for:

- paper review
- implementation planning

# Workflow

1. Field Overview

Identify:

- primary field
- adjacent fields

---

2. Historical Evolution

Explain:

- early paradigm
- major transitions
- current paradigm

---

3. Current Landscape

Analyze:

- active topics
- dominant approaches
- competing methodologies
- major challenges

---

4. Technology Routes

Compare major technical routes.

Explain:

- strengths
- weaknesses
- maturity

---

5. Future Trends

Estimate:

- short-term trends
- mid-term trends
- long-term trends

Separate evidence from speculation.

---

6. Research Guidance

Provide:

- recommended skills
- important foundations
- promising directions
- saturated directions

# Output Style

Focus on:

Evolution
→ Landscape
→ Trends
→ Strategy
